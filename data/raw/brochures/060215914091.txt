Welcome to Connecting Waters Charter - East Bay, where our mission is to provide a holistic and challenging educational environment. We focus on core literacy and numeracy, ensuring all students have a solid academic foundation. Beyond the core subjects, our school is a hub for creativity, offering rich programs in the arts and humanities that encourage self-expression and critical thinking. We offer various athletic opportunities and physical education programs to promote a healthy and active lifestyle. With a student-to-teacher ratio of 11.4:1, we prioritize personalized attention and academic support for every individual.
